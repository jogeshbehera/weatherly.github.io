About

Hello, I am Jogesh Behera, a Computer Science and Engineering student pursuing my education at Trident Academy of Technology. As part of my academic journey in the 7th semester, I am working on an exciting project - a Weather website. This project serves as a minor project that aligns with my coursework and provides an opportunity to apply the knowledge and skills acquired during my studies. The purpose of the weather website is to provide users with accurate and real-time weather information. From temperature and humidity to wind speed and air quality, the website aims to deliver a comprehensive weather forecast to keep users informed and prepared for their day.
